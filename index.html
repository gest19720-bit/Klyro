FAQ

Questions, answered.

Can't find what you're looking for? Reach us on WhatsApp any time.

What currencies does Klyro support?
Klyro supports 150+ currencies. Set a primary currency for your dashboard — GHS, NGN, USD, or EUR — and log transactions in any other. Conversion rates update in real time.
How secure is my financial data?
We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest, with isolated per-user storage, so your financials stay private and yours alone.
What is the Klyro AI Finance Coach?
A conversational financial agent trained to read spending patterns and cashflow trends inside the app — offering budgeting targets and flagging redundant charges.
Can I customize the visual interface?
Yes — Klyro's glass design is fully themeable. Personal and Corporate plan users can choose curated color palettes and light/dark adaptations.
