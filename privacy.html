Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 28, 2026

At Klyro ("we," "our," or "us"), we take your financial privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Klyro personal finance tracker application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when using Klyro:

  • Financial Data: Income, expenses, transaction descriptions, categories, and dates that you manually enter into the app.
  • Account Information: Email address and any information you provide during registration.
  • Waitlist Data: If you join our waitlist, we collect your name and email address.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Klyro service
  • To generate AI-powered insights and financial recommendations
  • To communicate with you about your account and updates
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To process waitlist registrations and notify you of launch

3. Data Storage and Security

Your financial data is stored locally on your device using 256-bit AES encryption. Key security measures include:

  • All data is encrypted before storage using industry-standard AES-GCM encryption
  • Passwords are hashed using SHA-256 with unique salts
  • Session tokens are stored separately from persistent data
  • We never transmit your financial data to external servers

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may use third-party services for analytics (anonymized data only) and email communications.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Klyro uses minimal cookies necessary for:

  • Session management and authentication
  • Remembering your theme preferences
  • Essential site functionality

We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: You can view all your data within the app at any time
  • Export: You can export your data as CSV at any time
  • Delete: You can clear all data from the app at any time + delete your account
  • Portability: Your data belongs to you and can be exported

7. Children's Privacy

Klyro is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. International Users

Klyro is operated from Ghana. If you access the service from outside Ghana, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in Ghana.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
